Setup EmailJS:

Sign up at https://www.emailjs.com (free tier)
Create an Email Service and copy the Service ID
Create an Email Template with these variables: from_name, to_email, no_clicks, selected_date, selected_food, selected_dessert, selected_activities
Copy your Public Key from Account settings
Replace the placeholder values in lastpage.html
